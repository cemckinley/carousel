Carousel Widget Class

Infinite auto rotate carousel with grouping, prev/next buttons, no pagination

Carousel rotates on its own, until the user interacts with it. Rotating is paused on window blur. You can move in either direction infinitely. Numeric pagination not recommended for infinite carousels, as it creates a strange user experience.

Finite carousel with pagination and grouping

No auto rotation. Slides are grouped into sets of how ever many are visible in the viewport. Navigation moves the slides in sets, and counts for sets instead of individual slides. When you run out of slides in one direction, a 'disabled' class is added to prev/next arrows accordingly.

Finite carousel with pagination and no grouping

More than one slide is visible at a time, but navigation acts on only one slide at a time, and pagination counts each slide. Since more than one slide is visible, an 'active' class is applied to each visible slide in the pagination.
